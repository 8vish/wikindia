How It Works

Welcome to the Persona 5 Fusion Calculator, your ultimate companion in navigating the complex world of persona fusion. Let's dive into how this tool works and how you can use it to enhance your Persona 5 experience.

Usage Guide

The Persona 5 Fusion Calculator is designed to simplify the persona fusion process, making it easier for you to create powerful personas for your journey. Here's a step-by-step breakdown of how to use this tool:

1. Persona List

Click on the "Persona" tab to access the complete list of personas in the game. Use the search bar to find specific personas quickly. You can also sort and filter personas based on various attributes such as level, arcana, and elemental values.

2. Skills List

Navigate to the "Skills" tab to explore a comprehensive list of skills available in Persona 5. Filter skills based on type, name, cost, and more to identify the perfect skills for your fusion strategy.

3. DLC Settings

Head to the "Settings" tab to configure your owned DLC personas. Check the checkboxes for the DLC personas you have, and click "Save" to apply your DLC settings for personalized fusion results.

4. Persona Details

Click on a persona's name in the list to access detailed information. Explore the persona's stats, elemental affinities, skills, and learn about its location in Mementos. This information is crucial for planning your fusion strategy.

5. Fusion Information

Discover fusion recipes for the selected persona. View both fusion-to and fusion-from recipes to plan your fusion strategies. Utilize the filter and search functions to find specific recipes tailored to your needs.

Tips and Reminders

Rare Personas

Note that rare persona fusion is influenced by your persona's current level. Be mindful of this when attempting fusions for rare personas. Consider the level of your persona to optimize your chances of obtaining the desired rare persona.

DLC Persona Configuration

Ensure accurate fusion results by configuring the DLC personas you own in the "Settings" tab. By doing this, you tailor the fusion calculations to your specific collection of personas, providing more relevant and personalized results.

Skill Card Negotiation and Fusion

Identify skills obtainable through negotiation and fusion by referring to the respective columns in the skills list. This information helps you strategize and plan for acquiring specific skills, enhancing the versatility and effectiveness of your personas.

How Fusion Works in Persona 5

Fusion in Persona 5 follows familiar rules from Persona 3 and 4, but with some additional nuances:

There are rare personas (Treasure Demons) that, when fused with a normal persona X, result in another persona one or two ranks above or below X's current level.

  • Notably, the outcome depends on the rare persona and X's arcana, meaning it's not fixed but varies based on X's current level.
  • Rare personas include Regent, Queen's Necklace, Stone of Scone, Koh-i-Noor, Orlov, Emperor's Amulet, Hope Diamond, and Crystal Skull.
  1. When two rare personas are fused, the normal fusion formula is applied.
  2. Rare personas cannot be the result of fusion.
  3. If a fusion results in a rare persona, it is skipped.
  4. 3-way fusion is no longer present.
  5. There are DLC personas. If you don't own a DLC persona, it's skipped during fusion.
  6. The calculator assumes you own no DLC personas by default (you can adjust this here). Also, for rare fusions, it assumes your persona is at the base level.

Note on Fusion with Judgement Arcana

Contrary to the official guide, fusing a persona of the Judgement arcana with personas of Justice/Strength/Chariot/Death arcana is not possible in the game.
